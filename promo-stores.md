### Promotional Text (<=170 chars)
Stream WPFW 89.3 FM live. See current show info, set a sleep timer, and support the station—right from the app.

### Description
WPFW 89.3 FM is Washington, D.C.’s home for “Jazz and Justice.” Stream the station live, get current show information, and enjoy a simple, accessible player designed for the community that WPFW has served since 1977 as a listener-supported Pacifica Network station. Use the side menu to access station archives, schedule, and playlists.

What you can do
- Stream WPFW live with reliable background playback
- Control audio from the Lock Screen and Control Center (iOS)
- See now-playing show information and artwork
- Set a full-screen Sleep Timer with presets and minute slider
- Support WPFW via the in-app Donate screen
- Explore Pacifica apps and services in a curated grid
- Access station archives, schedule, and playlists from the side menu
- Stay informed with graceful offline alerts and recovery
- Screen reader-friendly controls and live announcements

Why WPFW
Founded in 1977 and part of the Pacifica Foundation, WPFW is a community-powered station dedicated to jazz, blues, world music, and public affairs—amplifying underrepresented voices across the D.C., Maryland, and Virginia region.

Notes
- Background audio and Lock Screen controls supported.
- External links (e.g., donations) open in your default browser.
